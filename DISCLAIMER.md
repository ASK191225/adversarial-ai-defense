## Research Purpose

This repository implements adversarial patch attack and defense techniques
for computer vision models as a **security research pipeline**. It was built
to study, understand, and defend against adversarial threats to vision-based
systems — including autonomous vehicles, industrial sensors, and surveillance
infrastructure.

Both the attack (patch generation) and defense (ensemble detection) components
are included intentionally: you cannot build robust defenses without
understanding and replicating the attacks.
